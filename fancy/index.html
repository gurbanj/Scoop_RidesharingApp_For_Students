Reliability

All payments are made online in advance, to make sure they are committed to taking the ride. Drivers get paid after the ride is completed and all members are protected by our cancellation policies.

Affordability & Sustainability

A more efficient way of travelling from hotspot to hotspot. By scooping a ride, you also contribute to increase in vehicle occupancy, which offsets your carbon footprint with affordable ways to pay with either visa or mastercard.

Safety

All drivers of scoop undergo verification process of their profiles to make sure their license, email, phone, credit card and bank account are verified by our staff. Our support team is available 7 days a week and performs regular checks.

Get started with scoop in no time and have no regrets!

Economic Impact: Reducing car ownership costs. Offering a competitive means of transport for travellers. Building economic value by connecting universities & hotspots of Ontario. Helping smaller communities travel where public transport is unavailable.

Social Impact: Building a community based on the idea of sharing. Educating people on the need for shared mobility. Creating friendships by connecting people through ridesharing. Providing accessible travel for all.

Environmental Impact: Increasing occupancy rates by filling empty seats in cars. Forging partnerships with mobility organizations to incentivize shared transportation. Reducing carbon emissions by keeping cars off the road.
